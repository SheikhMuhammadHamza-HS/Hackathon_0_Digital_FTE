# Company Handbook

## AI Employee System

### Overview
This document serves as the handbook for the AI Employee system operations.

### Services
- **Email Processing**: Automatic email responses and drafting
- **Social Media Management**: X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram
- **Accounting**: Odoo ERP integration for invoicing
- **WhatsApp**: Business messaging automation

### Approval Workflow
1. Drafts are created in `Pending_Approval/`
2. Review drafts for accuracy
3. Move approved drafts to `Approved/`
4. System automatically sends approved items
